# Hi, I'm Justina 👋

I'm an environmental data scientist and Code4Food Security Fellow
(Blossom Academy | WFP Ghana | KOICA), with a background in
Wildlife and Range Management (BSc, KNUST) and over a decade of
field research in Ghana's ecosystems — from savanna woodlands at
Mole National Park to climate-vulnerable coastal communities in
the Volta Delta.

My work sits at the intersection of environmental science and
machine learning — using data to understand how climate, soil,
and water shape agricultural and ecological outcomes.
